Tofu Fried Noodles Recipe

Description

A quick and satisfying plant-based stir-fry packed with protein and vibrant vegetables. These chewy noodles are tossed in a savory garlic-ginger sauce with crispy pan-fried tofu for a delightful texture in every bite.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz stir-fry noodles (hokkien, udon, or rice noodles)
  • 1 block (14 oz) extra-firm tofu, pressed and cubed
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 large carrot, julienned
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sriracha (optional for heat)
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch mixed with 1 tablespoon water
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Prepare the noodles according to the package instructions, drain, and set aside.
  2. Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a large wok over medium-high heat. Add the tofu cubes and fry until golden brown on all sides, then remove from the pan.
  3. Add the remaining oil to the wok and stir-fry the broccoli and carrots for 3–4 minutes until tender-crisp.
  4. In a small bowl, mix the soy sauce, rice vinegar, sriracha, and cornstarch slurry.
  5. Return the tofu and noodles to the wok, pour the sauce over everything, and toss constantly for 1–2 minutes until the sauce thickens and coats the noodles evenly.
